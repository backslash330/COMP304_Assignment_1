Below are my Grandmothers recipes

I hope you enjoy them!

Beef Curry

Instructions

Cut the meat in slices. Cook it then fry onions, chillie, and tomatoes and add the masala and cool till tender.

Liver

Instructions

Clean and cut the liver. Grind the above to a paste. Fry some onions. Then put the masala in. After add the liver and 2 spoons of vinegar and 4 spoons of thick coconut juice.

Bibic

Instructions

Prepare sugar part when cool. Add eggs (yellow) then add the flour, coconut juice, vanilla and nutmeg. Bake layer by layer with ghee in between
